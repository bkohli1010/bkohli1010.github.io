Home Charging

Our variety of home charging options allow your drivers to continue to take their work vehicles home every day to help optimize uptime. Ford Pro E-Telematics can be used to provide charging reports and to reimburse drivers for business energy expenses.

Public Charging

With the ability to access over 70,000 plugs on the BlueOval™ Charge Network—the largest public charging network from coast to coast—along with mobile tools to help you find where to charge on longer routes3, your fleet can stay powered on the road.
