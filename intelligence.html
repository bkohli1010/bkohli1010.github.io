Ford Pro™ Intelligence

A Little Data Delivers Big Results

Large or small, your fleet is a big deal. That’s why we’ve created Ford Pro Intelligence, innovative software solutions that use the power of data to empower your fleet. It works by providing you with all the information — from vehicle health, driver behavior and vehicle location — you need to make real-time decisions that can help keep your drivers safe and your vehicles running smoothly.

Ford Pro Telematics Essentials

Available at no cost, Ford Pro Telematics Essentials provides real-time vehicle health alerts through engine diagnostic codes, recall information and more. What’s more, you also get planned maintenance tracking and, soon, remote lock/unlock capability features that can add efficiencies to your fleet management and a little bit of convenience for your drivers. Complimentary with the purchase of any Ford commercial vehicle, Ford Pro Telematics Essentials is ready to go when you are.

Ford Pro Telematics

The most important tool you never knew you needed, Ford Pro Telematics includes everything from the Essentials with added features like vehicle security, in-vehicle coaching,2 driver behavior insights, fuel usage and a mobile app for drivers. Every Ford Pro vehicle comes connectivity-ready with an embedded modem designed to work with Ford Pro Intelligence. The best part? It also works with any non-Ford vehicles of your fleet that are equipped with a plug-in device (PID).

Ford Pro E-Telematics

Ford Pro E-Telematics makes it easy to stay connected to the charging and maintenance needs of your all-electric vehicles. Delivered at no-charge for the first three years of your all-electric Ford fleet vehicle ownership, E-Telematics includes all the standard Telematics data, plus it monitors the charging status of every Ford all-electric vehicle in your fleet, in real-time, across depot, public and home charging solutions.3
